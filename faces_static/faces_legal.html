FACES Database Release Agreement

The FACES database is made available to researchers on a case-by-case (i.e., person-by-person and study-by-study) basis. To obtain access to the database, the researcher must sign the FACES Database Release Agreement contained in the application form and send it to the FACES Technical Agent, thereby agreeing to observe the restrictions listed in this document and to inform all other persons/researchers involved in the study about the restrictions. There is no charge for obtaining access to the FACES database.
